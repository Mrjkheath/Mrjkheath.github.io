What is Dimensional Analysis?

Dimensional Analysis also known as unit conversion, is a huge topic within general chemistry. Math is used all throughout chemistry, and luckily the math for unit conversions is fairly trivial. The table above is based off the metric system with SI units in mind.

You'll notice that all of the above Mutiples are in powers of ten. This makes converting between each base a simple movement of decimal place. If you were to enter 1 in the box above, you would be given an idea of what that number looks like at different powers of ten.
