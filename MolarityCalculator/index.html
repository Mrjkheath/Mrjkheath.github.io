Molarity Calculator

Calculating Molarity generally requires 3 steps:

  1. Calculate the moles of solute in the solution
  2. Calculate the amount of solution in liters
  3. The moles of solute is then divided by the liters of solution

Often times problems contain grams of a solute.
If this is the case, the grams must be converted to moles.
This is simply converted by dividing the grams given in
the problem, by the molar mass of the solute itself.
